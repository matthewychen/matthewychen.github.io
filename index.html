I am a first-year student in Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo, where I have worked with Profs. Jure Leskovec and Carlos Guestrin so far. Before this, I spent a lovely year learning under Prof. Zachary Lipton as a visiting researcher at Carnegie Mellon University. I did my undergrad in CS at IIT Delhi, where I was the President's Gold Medalist for the class of 2022.

I am interested in deep learning research, with a recent focus on foundation models. In my past work, I have enjoyed forays into noisy data learning, graph ML, neuro-symbolic AI, and program analysis.
